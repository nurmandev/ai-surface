Staff Presales Engineer, Hivemind

ai jobs Machine learning jobs ai engineering

Founded in 2015, Shield AI is a venture-backed deep-tech company with the mission of protecting service members and civilians with intelligent systems. Its products include the V-BAT and X-BAT aircraft, Hivemind Enterprise, and the Hivemind Vision product lines. With nine offices and facilities across the U.S., Europe, the Middle East, and the Asia-Pacific, Shield AI's technology actively supports operations worldwide. For more information, visit www.shield.ai. Follow Shield AI on LinkedIn, X, Instagram, and YouTube.

The Presales Engineer will provide technical expertise and support to customers before the sale and during the deployment of Shield AI's Hivemind products. This role involves working closely with customers to understand their mission requirements and demonstrating how Shield AI's solutions meet those needs. You will play a key role in ensuring successful product integration for AI and autonomy applications throughout the presales process.

What You'll do:

  • Contribute to White Papers, Request For Information Responses, Contract review, and Proposals.
  • Generate and deliver technical briefings tailored to customer needs.
  • Become a user of the Hivemind enterprise software stack and an expert in Hivemind Solutions.
  • Support the development and execution of demos.
  • Develop and maintain technical documentation and training materials.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues with Hivemind demos.
  • Collaborate with the engineering team to address customer feedback and improve products.
  • Visit customers on site globally to support presales activities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and refine prompts for ChatGPT, Midjourney, and DALL·E.
  • Collaborate with content, product, and AI research teams.
  • Maintain a library of reusable prompt templates and patterns.
  • Conduct A/B testing on different prompt styles to improve accuracy.
  • Stay updated with AI model changes and new prompt engineering techniques.
  • Analyze output performance and maintain consistency in brand tone.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 3+ years of experience working in a startup environment
  • Experience in the defense aviation, autonomy, or robotics industry
